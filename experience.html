Work Experience

Workiva

I am currently employed as an intern at Workiva, and have been since February 2016. In my position, I work in a small team of myself, one other intern, and a senior architect on an internal tool to provide vital information on the health of services and projects that other members of the organization use and contribute to every day. I am able to work on both the server and client side code which allows me to have a great diversified skill set in both server based coding and UI development.

Skills: Python, Dart, React, Flux, and Google App Engine. Agile development and professionalism.

Final Product: The product is still in development, but we have monthly product reviews in which I and my team present the new features that we have created to a large portion of the organization.

From this experience I have learned so much in such a small amount of time. I have learned a huge amount about the Agile Development process, as well as more technical skills with languages such as Python and Dart and tools such as Google App Engine, Git, and various build and deployment tools.

I had a lot of autonomy in this position, which allowed me to figure out things for myself and learn from my mistakes.

Skills: Java, Selenium, XML, XPath.

Final Product: The final project that I lef the company with was a full test suite that would fully test the site, including the database and various functions of the site.

From this experience I was able to learn the importance of automated testing and design of test systems, as well as how to properly parse XML documents with a certain level of tolerance and flexibility.
